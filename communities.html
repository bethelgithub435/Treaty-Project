First Nations in Treaty 1

The following First Nations are part of Treaty 1:

  • Peguis First Nation
  • Brokenhead Ojibway Nation
  • Long Plain First Nation
  • Roseau River Anishinaabe First Nation
  • Sandy Bay Ojibway Nation

Treaty 1 Today

Treaty 1 continues to shape relationships today. Some modern examples include urban reserves and economic development projects.

Examples include the Kapyong Urban Reserve and businesses owned by Treaty 1 communities. These projects support economic growth and self-determination for First Nations.
